# Tomorrow Meeting Plan With Yael

## Opening frame

"I want to test whether an AI assistant can help us move from idea -> palette-aware design directions -> critique -> next questions. Not final art, not replacing your hand. Just a structured design assistant."

## Demo flow

1. Show the text-only palette DB idea.
2. Show the intake form.
3. Run or describe the agent loop.
4. Look at 3 concept directions.
5. Ask Yael what feels useful/useless.
6. Let her change one constraint and rerun.

## Questions for Yael

- Which part of your process is slowest: concept, palette, sketch, client approval, scaling, or field execution?
- Would you rather receive image-like proposals or buildable region maps?
- How many colors do you typically want in one large public mosaic?
- What are the color families you have in abundance?
- How do you decide shard size in text, faces, sky, borders, and landscape?
- Do you use full-size printouts, projectors, chalk outlines, or freehand drawing?
- What should the entrance stone communicate emotionally?
- What must not appear?
- What Hebrew text must be readable from the road?
- What would make this tool annoying or creatively deadening?

## What to watch for

If she reacts to the questions more than the generated images, that is still success. It means the loop understands the process.
